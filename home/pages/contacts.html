Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a service from GraceTech?

Simply fill in the contact form above or send us an email directly. Tell us what you need and we will get back to you within 24 hours to discuss how we can help.

Do you work with clients outside Uganda?

Yes! While we are based in Kampala, Uganda, we work with clients remotely across Africa and beyond. Distance is never a barrier to building something great together.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website can take 1–2 weeks while a full application may take 4–8 weeks. We always discuss timelines clearly before starting.

Do you offer mentorship for beginners in tech?

Absolutely! STEM mentorship is one of our core missions. We especially welcome girls and young people from underserved backgrounds. Reach out and let's talk about how we can support your journey.

How can I apply for a job at GraceTech?

Visit our Jobs page to see all open positions and fill in the application form. We review every application carefully and will be in touch if you are a good fit for the team.

What makes GraceTech different from other tech companies?

GraceTech was built from a story of resilience and purpose. We do not just build technology — we build it to mean something. Every project, every hire and every mentorship session is driven by a mission bigger than profit.
