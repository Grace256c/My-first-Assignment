My Story

The Girl Who Said Yes

Grace Nakiyemba is the Founder and CEO of GraceTech Solutions and a software engineering scholar at Groundbreaker Talents, with a background rooted in resilience, purpose, and an unshakeable belief in the power of technology to transform lives.

An alumna of Code Queen, Grace grew up in rural Uganda as the firstborn of seven children raised by a single mother — a beginning that could have defined her limits, but instead became the foundation of her drive.

Once intimidated by the very computers she now builds with, Grace discovered her passion for technology in high school and refused to let financial barriers or limited access extinguish it. Today, she is actively building her expertise across software development and cybersecurity, with a long-term vision of strengthening digital security infrastructure in emerging communities across Africa.

Beyond writing code, Grace is on a mission to dismantle the barriers that keep girls from underserved backgrounds away from STEM — being the visible proof that it is possible. She believes the first step into technology requires only the courage to say yes before feeling fully ready.
